Biography

I am currently a Research Fellow at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), working with Prof. Wei Yang Bryan Lim and Prof. Cyril Leung in Midori Lab. I received my Ph.D. degree in 2025 from Beijing Jiaotong University, supervised by Prof. Jiqiang Liu and Prof. Wei Wang. My research mainly focuses on Trustworthy AI, specifically poisoning attacks and defense in distributed deep learning, multimodal LLMs, and multi-agent systems.
